My Session 4

Jake's Magical Sandwich

  • 1 talking French bread
  • 3 slices of cheese, preferably with personality
  • 1 singing tomato
  • 2 slices of bacon that can dance

Instructions:

  1. Ask the bread nicely to become the bread of destiny.
  2. Have a deep conversation with the cheese until it decides to join the adventure.
  3. Convince the tomato to sing its heart out.
  4. Teach the bacon some smooth moves.
  5. Assemble the ingredients into a sandwich and wait for the magic to happen.
